The Rapid7 Extension Library

I was the lead designer on the Rapid7 Extension Library, a marketplace for Rapid7 security capabilities. During my time at Rapid7 I have helped to turn the Extension Library from a MVP into a fully fledged marketplace, with a full redesign of the homepage, complete with personalised recommendations.

InsightConnect

InsightConnect is Rapid7's primary SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation and Response) product. It gives organisations the ability to automate complicated security tasks which gives their security professions more coverage in securing their environment.

My role involves talking to customers, and finding out what use cases they would like to have available to them in the product, and turning those conversations into fully fledged features.
